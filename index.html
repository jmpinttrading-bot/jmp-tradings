About Us

Established in 2022, JMP International Fish Trading has rapidly grown into a trusted name in the global seafood distribution network. We specialize in bridging the gap between bounty seas and international markets, delivering excellence on every voyage.

With an unwavering commitment to sustainability, total cold-chain integrity, and rigorous quality assurance protocols, we ensure that every single delivery retains optimal freshness, superior texture, and nutritional value from catch to destination.
